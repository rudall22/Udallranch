Our Story

The Ranch

Nestled in the high country of Arizona's White Mountains, Udall Ranch is a family operation built on a simple belief: cattle raised right produce beef that tastes right.

Our herd grazes on open Arizona pastures, living the way cattle were meant to live. We know our animals by name, manage our land with care, and take pride in every cut we send to your family's table.

  • No growth hormones or unnecessary antibiotics
  • Pasture-raised on Arizona's high-country grass
  • Family owned and operated — you're buying direct
  • Processed at a USDA-inspected facility
  • Custom-cut and vacuum sealed for your freezer
